  • Pundits
    • October 2015 - Present

    Pundits is an on-demand mobile app that brings students and tutors together. Students can easily submit a homework question and watch their question be answered in real-time via livestream by tutors on the platform. Pundits has made tutoring more convenient than ever for both students and tutors. Our team built a mobile application that interact together seamlessly through the Pundits app. The Student snaps a photo of their homework.   • AgePath
    • August 2015 - January 2016

    AgePath connects families-in-need with eldercare providers. We’ve enabled Eldercare providers to place a custom booking widget on their personal site, schedule availability for their employees, and track bookings through a custom back-end CRM platform. Consumers can find the closest eldercare providers in their zipcode and book appointments instantly for their loved ones through AgePath. One of the biggest insights we found was that consumers are 19 times more likely to commit to a booking when a provider has an online reservation system. We created a real-time booking software, marketing network, and care navigation platform. Consumer-facing site that serves as a Eldercare Resource Tool to help users find the best and Providers of elder care can quickly and easily sort/manage their client contact list with the custom CRM service we built with AgePath.


  • Mooch
    • June 2015 - September 2016

    Mooch is making collaborating with students easier. Mooch puts the classroom in your pocket, and allows students to share notes, create study groups, and easily connect with classmates. The team at Mooch recognized a huge problem at schools across the country—collaborating with other students in your classroom is difficult and inefficient with current technology. Working, collaborating, and sharing with those around you means better test score for everyone, and who doesn’t want that? Our development team chose to create a single-page app built on Meteor.js. What makes this framework a step above the rest is it’s super-smooth interface. Mooch’s easy-to-learn and easier-to-master interface takes the focus away from the learning curve and puts the emphasis on learning in the classroom. Colab initiated the planning of the project and executed development, and collaborated with the Mooch team on the design and UX. Once we had a design that could be aligned with our priorities, Colab took over control of development and executed the creation of Mooch.
